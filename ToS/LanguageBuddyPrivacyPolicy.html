LanguageBuddy Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the LanugageBuddy (“Bot”) collects, uses, and discloses your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

When you use a command, we may collect information about the command you used, and the time and date you used it. We will collect the data you input to the command and your basic user information such as username and nickname in a server.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve the Bot
  • Provide you with the features and functionality of the Bot
  • Respond to your requests
  • Personalize your experience
  • Comply with legal requirements

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose your information to:

  • Third-party service providers who help us operate the Bot
  • Law enforcement or other government officials if required by law

5. Data Retention

We will retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Bot’s features and functionality. We will also retain your information as necessary to comply with legal requirements.

6. Your Choices

We do not store any data in our server. If you need a copy of the conversation history, please reach out to Discord at support@discord.com.

7. Security

7.1 We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7.2 The following partners might process your data:

  • Cloudflare (for hosting)
  • Google (for all command’s AI integration)
  • Discord (for sending you the info you wish it to send and get the required data)

7.3 We value your privacy. Only developers can access the required data for development purposes only.

8. Children’s Privacy

The Bot is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are under 13, you may not use the bot.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. The updated Privacy Policy will be effective upon posting on the Bot’s website or GitHub repository.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about these Terms, please use one of the following ways to contact the ThunderBot team:
