ThunderBot Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the ThunderBot (“Bot”) collects, uses, and discloses your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Command Usage: When you use a command, we may collect information about the command you used, and the time and date you used it.

2.2 Mod Story Telling (/modstory): We do not collect any personal information when you use this command.

2.3 Talk with Gemini (/Gemini): We may collect the text you provide to the command and your Discord username. This information is required to generate responses and improve the Bot’s performance. This information will also be sent to Google Cloud AI API since Gemini is a product of Google.

2.4 Detect Suspicious Users (/sus): We will collect your username.

2.5 User Information (/user): We may collect your Discord username, the time you joined Discord, and the ID when you use this command. This information is used to retrieve public information about the user from the Discord API.

2.6 Define Word (/define): We may collect the word your query and your Discord username. This information is required to retrieve definitions from Urban Dictionary and improve the Bot’s performance. We will also send the result of the lookup from Urban Dictionary and your Discord username to Cloudflare AI to filter not safe for work (NSFW) contents in order to ensure that the result is safe for work

2.7 Play Rickroll (/bestsong): We do not collect any personal information when you use this command.

  1. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve the Bot
  • Provide you with the features and functionality of the Bot
  • Respond to your requests
  • Personalize your experience
  • Comply with legal requirements

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose your information to:

  • Third-party service providers who help us operate the Bot
  • Law enforcement or other government officials if required by law

5. Data Retention

We will retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Bot’s features and functionality. We will also retain your information as necessary to comply with legal requirements.

6. Your Choices

We do not store any data in our server. If you need a copy of the conversation history, please reach out to Discord at support@discord.com.

7. Security

7.1 We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7.2 The following partners might process your data:

  • Cloudflare (for hosting)
  • Cloudflare AI (for /modstory command, /define command and /sus command)
  • Google (for /Gemini command)
  • Discord (for sending you the info you wish it to send and get the required data)
  • Urban Dictionary (for getting the definition of a word you sent using the /define command)

7.3 We value your privacy. Only developers can access the required data for development purposes only.

8. Children’s Privacy

The Bot is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are under 13, you may not use the bot.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. The updated Privacy Policy will be effective upon posting on the Bot’s website or GitHub repository.
